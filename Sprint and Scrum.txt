Sprint/Scrum: 
A short, time-boxed period where a set amount of work is complete. 
Constant meetings are made to assess progress.

Advantage
	- Flexiable (able to review project and make changes when needed)
	- Able to gain client's feedback
	
Disadvantage
	- Can lead to delay if a team member is unable to complete their task
	- If a team members leaves, it can cause a huge impact on the project
Reasons to use this methodology:
	- Unlike waterfall methodology where it cannot review its completed 
	  stages the sprint/scrum can.
	- Able to view the progress of each team member (Provide support)
